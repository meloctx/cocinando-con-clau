About Clau

Clau (they/them) identifies as a Brown queer farmer who centers their work around relationship building with land, food, and herbs. They started the Instagram page @cocindandoconclau as a way to document their journey of cooking colorful food that feeds their heart, soul, and tummy.

They have been developing recipes for about 5 years now but they orginally learned how to cook from their mother since they were 10 years old. It has been important for them to continue traditional recipes that bring good memories of growing up harvesting their own fruits and vegetables.

Clau grew up in the Central Valley in a small town right off HWY 99 known as Delhi, California. They grew up in a farmworking family, where since birth they were out in the fields while their parents picked fruits and vegetables for big monoculuture food companies.
